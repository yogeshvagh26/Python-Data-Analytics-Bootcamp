## Learning Format

For every lesson, followed this structure:

1. Concept Explanation
2. Importance and Real-World Use Cases
3. Syntax and Rules
4. Step-by-Step Demonstration
5. Multiple Practical Examples
6. Hands-on Coding Exercises
7. Mini Quiz
8. Common Mistakes and Best Practices
9. Interview Questions
10. Assignment and Mini Project
11. Summary and Revision Notes
